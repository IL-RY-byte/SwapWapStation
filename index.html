How SwapWapStation Works

1. Donate & Catalog

Drop off items at collection points or register via app with details and stories

2. Assess & Match

Volunteers evaluate items for repair, reuse, or direct donation to IDPs

3. Exchange & Connect

Monthly swap events bring communities together - no money, just stories

4. Learn & Grow

Track environmental impact, earn certificates, build leadership skills
